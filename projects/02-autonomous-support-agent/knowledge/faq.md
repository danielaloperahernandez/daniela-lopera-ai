# Support FAQ

## How do I reset my password?

Open the login page, click "Forgot password", and enter your account email. You'll receive a
reset link valid for 30 minutes. If it doesn't arrive, check spam or try again after a minute.

## What are your support hours?

Our team answers Monday to Friday, 9am to 6pm (America/Bogota). Outside those hours the bot
can still answer common questions, and a human will follow up the next business day.

## How do I export my data?

Go to Settings -> Data -> Export. We generate a ZIP with your data in JSON and CSV and email
you a download link when it's ready (usually within a few minutes).

## Which plans are available?

We offer Free, Pro, and Team plans. Pro unlocks unlimited projects and priority support; Team
adds shared workspaces and SSO. You can change plans anytime from Settings -> Billing.

## How do I contact a human agent?

Just say "talk to a human" or ask anything related to billing or a complaint, and the request
is routed to our support team automatically.
